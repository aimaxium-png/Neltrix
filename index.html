// 06 — Clarity

COMMON QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know about our systems and process.

What exactly is included in a website build — just a landing page or a full site?
It depends on what your business needs. We build everything from single landing pages (great for restaurants, salons, or service businesses wanting a professional online presence) all the way to full multi-page websites with custom functionality. Every build includes mobile-responsive design, Google Maps integration, contact/booking forms, WhatsApp link, and deployment on a live domain. We'll scope exactly what you need on our first call.
How long does it take to build and go live?
A standard landing page is live in 3–5 business days. A full multi-page website typically takes 7–14 days depending on the complexity and how quickly you provide content (logo, text, images). We move fast — and once we have everything from your side, we don't sit on it.
Does this AI receptionist really sound like a human?
Yes. We use state-of-the-art ultra-low latency voice models. It handles interruptions, says "um" and "ah" naturally, and speaks with emotional intelligence. Most callers never realize they are speaking to an AI.
What other AI services do you offer beyond voice agents?
A lot more than voice. We build WhatsApp AI chatbots that handle customer queries and bookings, AI automation workflows using Zapier and n8n that connect your tools and eliminate repetitive manual tasks, lead scraping and qualification systems, Google Sheets data pipelines, and custom AI-powered tools tailored to your business. If it involves automating a process or handling communication without a human — we can build it.
I already use a CRM — can you integrate with it instead?
Yes. We can connect to most popular CRMs via Zapier, n8n, or direct API integrations. Whether you're on HubSpot, Zoho, Salesforce, or a custom system — we'll plug our AI and automation layer on top of what you already have rather than force you to switch platforms.
How does pricing work — is it one-time or a monthly fee?
Both, depending on the service. Website builds are one-time payments (starting at $1,499). AI voice agents and CRM management are monthly retainers ($499–$799/mo) because they require ongoing hosting, maintenance, and optimization. Automation workflows are priced per project (starting at $999 each). We're always transparent — no surprise fees, no locked contracts. You'll see the full breakdown before anything starts.
Is there a contract? What if I want to cancel?
No long-term contracts. Monthly services run month-to-month — cancel anytime with 7 days notice. We also offer a 30-day results guarantee: if we set up your system and you don't see measurable improvement in the first month, we'll work for free until you do. We're not interested in keeping clients who aren't getting value.
I'm not technical at all. Will I understand what's being built?
That's exactly why we exist. You don't need to understand the tech — you just need to understand the result. We explain everything in plain language, show you what's live before you pay, and handle all the technical side completely. Our clients are restaurant owners, salon owners, and real estate agents — not developers. If you can use WhatsApp, you can use what we build for you.
Can you handle multiple services at once — website AND AI AND CRM?
Yes — this is actually our all-in-one model. Most agencies only do one thing. We have a full-stack developer (custom websites and integrations) and an AI specialist (voice agents, automations, CRM) working together. You get a single point of contact for your entire digital system. Many clients start with a website, then add AI automation, then CRM — and it all connects seamlessly because one team built everything.
